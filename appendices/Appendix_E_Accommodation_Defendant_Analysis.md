### E.2 Defendant Type Distribution

Defendant type distribution for the Unified Dataset (N=3,193) is reported in Appendix A, Section A.5. Key interpretive notes:

- The counterintuitive pattern — public-entity defendants yielding lower plaintiff win rates than private defendants — may reflect sovereign immunity defenses, resource asymmetries, or the complexity of challenging institutional housing policies.
- Housing authorities are disproportionately targeted by pro se plaintiffs (69% of HA cases are pro se).
